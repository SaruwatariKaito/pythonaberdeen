Pythonistas of Aberdeen!

We are a group of Python programmers based in the granite city. If you are interested in the Python Programming language, this group is for you. All skill levels are welcome. We meet periodically at the pub, but we organise other events like dojos and talks.

When and Where

Python Aberdeen meets on the 4th Wednesday of each month usually at the pub for a casual beer. We'll organise other events like dojos and talks. The type of event and the place will be announced via twitter and the mailing list.
